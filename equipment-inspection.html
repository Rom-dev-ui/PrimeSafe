Our Proven Inspection Process

1

Pre-Inspection Planning

Review of equipment history, design specifications, and previous inspection reports to develop customized inspection plan

2

Non-Destructive Testing

Application of advanced NDT methods including UT, RT, PT, MT, and thermography based on equipment requirements

3

Structural Integrity Assessment

Detailed evaluation of stress points, corrosion rates, fatigue cracks, and material degradation

4

Safety Systems Verification

Testing of all safety interlocks, emergency stops, pressure relief devices, and warning systems

5

Comprehensive Reporting

Detailed report with findings, remaining life calculation, repair recommendations, and recertification documentation
