Professional Safety Audits for Kenyan Businesses

PrimeSafe's safety audits are comprehensive evaluations of your workplace safety systems, designed to identify potential hazards, assess compliance with Kenyan safety regulations, and recommend corrective actions. Our audits help prevent accidents, reduce liability, and create safer work environments.

Risk Identification

Systematic identification of potential workplace hazards

Compliance Verification

Assessment against OSHA Kenya and other regulatory standards

Performance Metrics

Measurement of safety program effectiveness

1

Pre-Audit Consultation

2

Documentation Review

Evaluation of safety policies, training records, and incident reports

3

On-Site Inspection

Report & Recommendations
