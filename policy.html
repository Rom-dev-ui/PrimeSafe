1. Introduction

PrimeSafe Inspections Ltd ("PrimeSafe", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1.1 Scope

This policy applies to information we collect through our website, mobile applications, email, and other electronic communications, as well as through our inspection services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, company details when you contact us or request services
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage details collected automatically
  • Inspection Data: Equipment details, inspection results, and compliance information related to our services
  • Payment Information: Billing details for service payments (processed through secure payment gateways)

2.1 Collection Methods

We collect information through website forms, service agreements, cookies, and during the provision of our inspection services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our inspection services
  • To process transactions and send invoices
  • To communicate with you about services, updates, and safety information
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To protect the rights and safety of our company, clients, and the public

3.1 Legal Basis

We process your data based on contractual necessity, legitimate business interests, legal compliance, and with your consent where required.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third parties who perform services on our behalf (payment processing, IT services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • Safety Compliance: With regulatory authorities as required by industrial safety regulations
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition

4.1 International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Kenya, where we ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses or other approved mechanisms.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Employee training on data protection

5.1 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Inspection records are typically retained for 10 years to comply with safety regulations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

6.1 Exercising Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users' movements, and gather demographic information.

7.1 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated effective date.
